    Cookie Policy

    Last updated: March 15, 2025

    What are cookies

    Cookies are small text files that the websites visited by the user send and store on their device (computer, tablet, smartphone), to be later retransmitted to the same sites on the next visit. Cookies allow the site to remember the user's actions and preferences (such as login data, preferred language, font size, other display settings, etc.) so that they do not have to be indicated again when the user returns to visit the same site or browses its pages.

    Types of cookies used

    Founders.it uses various types of cookies, each with a specific function. Technical cookies are essential for the proper functioning of the site and to allow the user to navigate and use the features made available. Without these cookies the site could not function properly. Functionality cookies allow the site to remember the choices made by the user to provide a customised version of the service. Analytics cookies, both first and third party, are used to collect aggregate information on the number of visitors and how they navigate the site.

    Third-party cookies

    The Founders.it site uses some third-party services that may install cookies on the user's device. In particular, statistical analysis services provided by Google Analytics are used, configured with IP address anonymisation, and newsletter management services provided by MailerLite. For each third-party service, the user is invited to consult the specific privacy notice of the provider, accessible through the links on this page. Founders.it does not have direct access to the data collected through these services and is not responsible for the privacy policies of the individual providers.

    Cookie duration

    The cookies used by the site have different durations depending on their type and purpose. Session cookies are automatically deleted when the browser is closed. Persistent cookies, on the other hand, remain stored on the user's device until they expire or are manually deleted. The duration of the persistent cookies used by the Founders.it site ranges from 30 days to 24 months, depending on the specific purpose of the cookie. The user can at any time delete cookies from their browser through its settings.

    How to manage cookies

    The user can manage cookie preferences through their browser settings. Most browsers allow you to accept or reject all cookies, to accept only certain types, or to be warned every time a site proposes the installation of a cookie. It is also possible to delete at any time the cookies already stored on the device. However, please note that disabling technical cookies may compromise the proper functioning of the site and prevent the user from accessing some features.

    Instructions for the main browsers

    To manage cookies in the main browsers you can consult the following official help pages. For Google Chrome: support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647. For Mozilla Firefox: support.mozilla.org/kb/cookies-information-websites-store-on-your-computer. For Safari: support.apple.com/guide/safari/sfri11471. For Microsoft Edge: support.microsoft.com/microsoft-edge/delete-cookies-in-microsoft-edge. Users are invited to periodically check these settings to ensure they match their preferences.

    Updates to the cookie policy

    This cookie policy may be subject to updates, also as a consequence of the evolution of applicable legislation or the addition of new services to the site. The user is invited to consult this page regularly to be informed of any changes. The date of the last update is always shown at the bottom of the page. In case of substantial changes, the Data Controller will inform users through a notice published on the site.

    Contact

    For any question or request for clarification relating to this cookie policy, the user can contact the Data Controller at privacy@founders.it. For more general information on the processing of personal data, please refer to the Privacy Policy section of the site. The Controller undertakes to respond to every request as soon as possible and in any case within the terms provided by applicable law on the protection of personal data.
